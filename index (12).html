No hardware needed — just a camera and your hands (or sticks)

Press Start to begin
Camera will appear here
🔊 Volume 85% ⚡ Sensitivity 20
How to play:
  • Click Start Camera and allow camera access when prompted.
  • Move your hands or drumsticks into the coloured zones.
  • Strike downward to trigger that drum — hover won't trigger it.
  • For the Kick, move your foot or hand low into that bottom zone.
  • Best with good lighting and a still background behind you.
  • Raise Sensitivity if zones aren't triggering; lower it to reduce false hits.
Air Drum Kit — 100% client-side, no API, no server. Drop index.html in any GitHub repo.
