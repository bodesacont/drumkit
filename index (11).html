How to play:
  • Click Start Camera and allow browser access when prompted.
  • Move your hands or drumsticks in front of the camera.
  • Position a hand inside a coloured zone, then strike downward to trigger that drum.
  • Works best with a still background and good lighting.
  • Adjust Sensitivity if sounds trigger too easily or not enough.
  • Two hands tracked independently — coloured dots show detected motion.

Tip for phones: Prop your phone against something so the front camera faces you, then play in front of it. Landscape mode gives more zone space.
